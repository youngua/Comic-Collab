DIALOGUES—

Spring 2020

MADE @ RISD

Dialogues is a game of telephone in the form of a comic between 45 students with varying backgrounds at the Rhode Island School of Design (RISD) in the Spring of 2020. Dialogues aimed to engage broadened interactions amongst individuals while embracing the playful, bizarre dialogue and community found with the mixing of people from different fields of interest and backgrounds. The process made evident how personalities and interests of individuals come out the way that they communicate and respond to the narratives presented to other people? How do multiple individuals bring their voices together to create a community through the interactions they have? How are we presenting & engaging in a narrative to contribute to a whole? How do our dialogues help us build together?

Each artist/designer was asked to create a singular page that continued the narrative of what happened before it. Due to limited time and the large number of people, two linear narratives were created simultaneously and were connected afterwards. The only restriction given was that the final output had to fit on a 7 x 5” page formatted for a 2 colour print.
